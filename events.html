                                 January 11, 2020: Meet the UNC Jump Rope Team                 

Join us at the start of the year for a chance to meet the UNC Jump Rope team and learn what Jump Ahead is all about! This event will be heald on UNC's campus in Woollen gym from 10am to 1pm on January 11. It is open to all students and parents interested in our program and how to get involved. No registration necessary.

                 February 5, 2020: After School Program at McDougle Middle School                                  

Join us for an after school snack activity at McDougle Middle School in Chapel Hill. We will be showing students how to make healthy snacks! Check out our Food & Exercise Videos page to see some of our past after school programs.
